Employee Initiatives

Employee Engagement Committee

The Employee Engagement Committee is a staff-run committee which works collectively to encourage staff; work together as a group; share fun ideas; enhance staff satisfaction and work effectiveness; and to bring clinics together. The Employee Engagement Committee helps to plan a number of employee initiatives, activities, and events. If you'd liked to get involved, please reach out to HR@naomedical.com!

Employee Scholarship Program

Nao Medical has recently launched a scholarship program, which gives partial tuition reimbursement to ten employees via a scholarship fund of approximately $30,000 per year. For more details on the program, please contact HR at HR@naomedical.com.

Peer Mentorship Program

Nao Medical is initiating a mentorship program for our employees, to enable them to buddy up with a mentor of their choice who aligns with their career goals. Whether the employee is currently in school or plans to attend in the near future, the peer mentorship program will help guide them into the right direction. Employee mentors could be from any career path, including medical assistants, nurse practitioners, patient assistants, adminstrators/managers, or MDs. If any employees are either interested in being mentored, or would like to volunteer as a mentor, please reach out to HR to get involved!

Provider Internship/Externship Program

Nao Medical's internship/externship program is being initiated at the Interfaith Medical Center Emergency Room. This program will allow all of the new grads coming into the company to shadow and follow other mid level providers or doctors for two weeks, with three shifts a week, to gain first hand experience and practice procedures before joining the clinic as a sole provider.

Employee Feedback & Company Goals

Employee satisfaction is very important to us at Nao Medical, and our goal is to be the best place for our employees to work. To help us measure our performance as a company and as an employer, we conduct regular eNPS studies. Thank you for your participation and sharing your honest feedback, as it helps us make Nao Medical better every day!
