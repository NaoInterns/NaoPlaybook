How to use the Nao Medical playbook

In this playbook you can learn about the story of Nao Medical, our founders, and the mission and values that are central to everything we do. There is essential information about our company, a useful directory, and other resources to help you on your path to success as part of the Nao Medical team.

We strive to provide the best patient care available, and looking after our patients is at the heart of everything we do. We've put together a thoughtful section on how we engage with, treat, and look after every patient who comes through our doors, which will be developed to include helpful resources and training guides.

For employees working at our offices, there are important sections that contain information regarding essential administration functions, useful contact information, and other helpful resources.

Finally, our detailed HR policies cover all of the essentials of your employment at Nao Medical, from daily schedules and time-off requests, to our standards of conduct and professionalism.
