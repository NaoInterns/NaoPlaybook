Step 1: Preparation

Action Steps

  • Send floor plans to Incubis Consultants (incubis.consultants@gmail.com)
  • Create a demographics sheet
  • Send a gift basket
  • Collect Temporary Signage

Floor Plans and Blueprints

For every location, a set of floor plans with dimensions and any other architectural notes should be sent to our architecture consultants. These should be accompanied by video walkthroughs/photos of the exterior and interior.

Collect Temporary Signage

Throughout the construction process, we want to let people know what to expect and get people excited. For this, we have temporary signage available and it's important to collect them and put them up early.

Demographics Sheet

Regardless of whether a location is new or old, we must assemble some information on the neighborhood. A demographics sheet should include the following information:

  • average age
  • median income
  • ethnic makeup
  • citizen %
  • Educational statuses
  • Population

By knowing this information, it will help Nao Medical become a part of the community and gear our operations to better suit the neighborhood.

Send a gift basket

Send gift baskets to neighbors and surrounding businesses to let them know what we stand for. Hand write a message that explains what we hope to accomplish.
